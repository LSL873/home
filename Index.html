Rules to be Followed in Tranfer Market
Rule No.1 : No Transfers Outside the Window

Players cannot be transferred unless the official Transfer Window is open.

* Transfer activity is strictly prohibited during the offseason or any period when the window is closed.
* Emergency exceptions (e.g., free agent signings or injury crises) require league approval.

Ensures fair competition and provides structured periods for clubs to plan and ne

Rule No.2 : Slot-Based Recruitment

Teams may only sign players to fill vacant slots in their squad. No team can exceed the maximum squad size.

* Each team has a predetermined squad size limit (e.g.,3 players).
* Transfers are only allowed to replace outgoing players or fill unoccupied slots.
* Any attempt to exceed the squad limit will result in voided transfers and possible penalties.

Prevents stockpiling of talent by wealthier teams and ensures a fair distribution of players across the league.

Rule No.3 : Budget Declaration and Spending Limits

Teams must disclose a set budget at the start of the Transfer Window. Exceeding this amount will result in severe penalties.

* The disclosed budget includes all spending on transfers, agent fees, and player wages during the window.
* If a team exceeds the declared amount: - **First Offense:** Heavy fines and a temporary transfer ban (e.g., one window).
- **Repeat Offenses:** Escalating penalties, including multi-window bans and point deductions.
* Teams are required to submit detailed financial records for review at the end of the window.

Ensures financial discipline, transparency, and fair competition while discouraging reckless spending.
